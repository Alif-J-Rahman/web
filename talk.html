What is Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. The game was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in the Java programming language (very cool we that we will be learning this). It is one of the most popular games of all time. In Minecraft. players expolore a blocky, 3D world with infitite terrain, and can discover the world and harvest it's resources to eventually beat the game by defeating the final boss: the Ender Dragon. Anyone of any age can play this game and have massive amounts of fun and get joy out of it. This is because Minecraft is a place where you can let your creativity run free... you can build massive structures, design arenas, roleplay, and overall you can do almost anything by yourself or with friends.

How did I find out about Minecraft

I think the way that I found out about Minecraft is very unique. The details are a bit fuzzy but I will try to convery my story as clearly as possible: I think in second grade, I was working on an assignment with a group. Now obviously we started chatting about random things other than the assignment since we were second graders. One friend told me if I know what "What does the Fox say" is. I told him I don't. He told me to go to this website I have never heard of, Youtube, and search it up there. So afterschool, jolly 'ole me decided to exactly that. However, I never even got to the music video. Instead, I watched an Annoying Orange video of "What does the sock say". This introduce me to Annoying Orange's channel and I began to watch it regularly. As you might have guessed, the channel uploaded a video about Minecraft and I was immediatly intrigued. Following the end of that video, I searched up Minecraft in Youtube and found tons of communities and content creators based around Minecraft and have been hooked on the game ever since.

My experience and why it is important to me

I wasn't really every into the single player side of Minecraft. Creativity really wasn't for me I guess. I immediately joined multiplier servers such as Mineplex and Hypixel. There I played mini games with myself and the friends I made along the way for most of my childhood. It was such a fun time and I appreciate that I was fortunate enough to experience it. Playing on those servers are one of my fondest memories as before I didn't really have a social life after school. But after discovering Minecraft, I got to spend time with multiple people of diverse backgrounds.
